# Baby Allergen Introduction Checklist

## Before you start
- Baby should be **well** that day.
- Do the **first exposure at home**, **morning or midday**, when baby will be awake for **about 2 hours** after.
- Do **only 1 new allergen at a time**.
- If tolerated, keep giving that food **2x/week** as our household rule.

| Done | Order | Allergen | Day 1 | Day 2 | Day 3 goal | Ongoing | Sample recipe |
|---|---:|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| ☐ | 1 | **Egg** | **1/4 tsp**, wait **10 min**, then **1/4 tsp** | **1 tsp** | **about 1/3 well-cooked egg** | **2x/week** | Hard-boiled egg mixed into oatmeal or applesauce |
| ☐ | 2 | **Peanut** | **1/4 tsp**, wait **10 min**, then **1/4 tsp** | **1 tsp** | **2 tsp** | **2x/week** | **2 tsp** smooth peanut butter + **2–3 tsp** warm water |
| ☐ | 3 | **Milk / Dairy** | **1/4 tsp**, wait **10 min**, then **1/4 tsp** | **1 tsp** | **2 tsp** | **2x/week** | Plain whole-milk yogurt |
| ☐ | 4 | **Wheat** | **1/4 tsp**, wait **10 min**, then **1/4 tsp** | **1 tsp** | **2 tsp** | **2x/week** | Wheat cereal or porridge |
| ☐ | 5 | **Soy** | **1/4 tsp**, wait **10 min**, then **1/4 tsp** | **1 tsp** | **2 tsp** | **2x/week** | Tofu mixed into oatmeal or puree |
| ☐ | 6 | **Sesame** | **1/4 tsp**, wait **10 min**, then **1/4 tsp** | **1 tsp** | **2 tsp** | **2x/week** | **2 tsp** tahini + a little warm water |
| ☐ | 7 | **Fish** | **1/4 tsp**, wait **10 min**, then **1/4 tsp** | **1 tsp** | **2 tsp** | **2x/week** | Salmon mixed into oatmeal or puree |
| ☐ | 8 | **Tree nuts** *(1 nut at a time)* | **1/4 tsp**, wait **10 min**, then **1/4 tsp** | **1 tsp** | **2 tsp** | **2x/week each nut** | Almond butter + warm water |
| ☐ | 9 | **Shellfish** | **1/4 tsp**, wait **10 min**, then **1/4 tsp** | **1 tsp** | **2 tsp** | **2x/week** | Shrimp mixed into oatmeal or puree |

## How to use this sheet
- **Day 1:** first tiny exposure.
- **Day 2:** bigger amount.
- **Day 3:** normal small serving.
- Start the **next new allergen on Day 4 or Day 5**.
- For **tree nuts**, do **one nut at a time**: almond first, then later repeat the same 3-day process for cashew, walnut, etc.
- The **order here is a practical household order**.

## Reaction guide
- **Usually okay to watch:** a **mild rash or redness only around the mouth**, with no other symptoms.
- **Stop the food and call the pediatrician:** swelling of lips/face/eyes, hives or welts on the body, vomiting, or baby becomes very unsettled soon after eating.
- **Get urgent help / call 911:** difficult or noisy breathing, wheeze, tongue swelling, pale/floppy, or baby seems suddenly limp/lethargic.
